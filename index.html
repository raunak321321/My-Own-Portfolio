Stock Prediction

An LSTM model capable of forecasting the upcoming day's opening stock price by leveraging data from the past 90 days, encompassing high, low, open, and close stock prices, coupled with the sentiment analysis of news pertaining to each respective day. This model effectively utilizes the sequence of historical data to generate predictions, where the input comprises prior days' information and the output yields the projected opening price for the current day.

Bus Booking Website

A comprehensive full-stack website designed to streamline the ticket booking process, featuring a robust user authentication system. The platform is highly user-friendly, offering seamless navigation for users to effortlessly reserve tickets. It provides the convenience of managing and accessing all previously booked tickets, checking the current status of bookings, and even obtaining contact information for bus drivers. Additionally, users have the flexibility to book multiple tickets simultaneously, enhancing their overall booking experience.
